Frequently Asked Questions

What is the adoption process?
The adoption process involves filling out an application, a screening process, and meeting the pet. Once approved, you can take your new pet home.
How much does it cost to adopt a pet?
Adoption fees vary depending on the type of pet. Generally, fees range from RWF50,000 to Rwf 100,000, covering vaccinations, spaying/neutering, and other medical costs.
What should I prepare before adopting a pet?
Ensure you have a safe space, food, water, bedding, toys, and time to dedicate to your new pet. Research the specific needs of the pet you plan to adopt.
Can I adopt if I have other pets?
Yes, but it's important to ensure your current pets are comfortable with a new addition. We can provide guidance on introducing new pets to your home.
